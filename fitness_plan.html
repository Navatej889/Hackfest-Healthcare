Fitness Plan for Weight Loss

Workout Plan

Monday: Cardiovascular exercise (e.g., brisk walking or jogging) for 30 minutes
Tuesday: Full-body strength training (e.g., bodyweight exercises or light weights) for 30 minutes
Wednesday: Rest or light activity (e.g., yoga or stretching)
Thursday: Cardiovascular exercise for 30 minutes
Friday: Full-body strength training for 30 minutes
Saturday: High-intensity interval training (HIIT) for 20 minutes
Sunday: Rest or light activity

Diet Plan

Breakfast: Whole-grain cereal with low-fat milk, a banana, and a handful of almonds
Mid-Morning Snack: Greek yogurt with berries
Lunch: Grilled chicken or tofu with quinoa and mixed vegetables
Afternoon Snack: Apple slices with peanut butter
Dinner: Baked salmon or lentils with brown rice and steamed broccoli
Evening Snack: Carrot sticks with hummus
Hydration: Drink plenty of water throughout the day, at least 8 glasses (64 ounces).

Remember to consult with a healthcare professional or a fitness/nutrition expert before starting any new exercise or diet program. Adjust the intensity and duration of exercises based on your fitness level, and ensure that your diet plan aligns with your individual health needs and dietary preferences.
