What is stress?

  • Stress is the result of our reactions to pressure or threats. It typically occurs when we feel helpless or uncontrollable in a circumstance. Stress may manifest in several ways, such as: On an individual level, such as when you are overburdened with obligations and find it difficult to handle them.

How to manage stress?

  • Create a schedule that includes regular exercise, give enough sleep a high priority, engage in mindfulness exercises like meditation, and ask for help from others when you need it to handle stress.
