A health management hub is a centralized platform that offers materials and tools to help people track and manage their health. Personal health records, wellness monitoring, appointment scheduling, health education, prescription management, advice on exercise and nutrition, telehealth services, health risk assessments, and community assistance are some of the elements that are commonly included. The goal is to provide a comprehensive solution for controlling many areas of health and well-being, albeit the exact features may differ.
