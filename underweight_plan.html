Weight Gain Plan

Workout Plan

Monday: Strength training focusing on major muscle groups (e.g., squats, deadlifts) for 30 minutes
Tuesday: Rest or light activity (e.g., walking)
Wednesday: Full-body strength training for 30 minutes
Thursday: Rest or light activity
Friday: Strength training with compound exercises (e.g., bench press, overhead press) for 30 minutes
Saturday: Rest or light activity
Sunday: Cardiovascular exercise for 20 minutes (optional)

Diet Plan

Breakfast: Whole-grain toast with peanut butter, banana, and a glass of whole milk
Mid-Morning Snack: Greek yogurt with honey and mixed berries
Lunch: Grilled chicken or tofu with quinoa, sweet potato, and mixed vegetables
Afternoon Snack: Nut and dried fruit trail mix
Dinner: Salmon or with brown rice, broccoli, and avocado
Evening Snack: Cottage cheese with pineapple
Before Bed: Casein protein shake or a small bowl of oatmeal with milk

It's essential to consume more calories than you burn to gain weight. Focus on nutrient-dense foods, include protein-rich sources, and stay hydrated. Adjust the workout intensity based on your fitness level, and consult with a healthcare professional or fitness expert for personalized guidance.
